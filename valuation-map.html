Investment Map

Pick one investment and see a clean visual map of peers, hedges, anchors, amplifiers, and other connected ideas.

Connection Mapper

We score nearby investments using shared themes, 90-day trend, volatility, sentiment, and a few anchor or hedge relationships, then place them into a visual map around the center idea.

Type an investment or choose from the dropdown, then build the map.

How To Read It

The center node is your selected asset. Nearby nodes are not recommendations by default. They are comparison points.

  • Theme Peer means the asset belongs to a similar market story or exposure cluster.
  • Momentum Peer means the recent trend and score profile look similar.
  • Hedge points to a ballast asset that can offset the main position's risk.
  • Anchor gives you a broad market reference so you can compare the pick against a bigger benchmark.
